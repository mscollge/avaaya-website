About INTREACT 2026

The International e-Conference on Interdisciplinary Research, Education and Advanced Collaborative Technologies (INTREACT 2026) is organized by Avaaya Infotech Private Limited, a MSME-Udyam and Startup India registered research services company.


INTREACT 2026 aims to bring together global researchers, academicians, educators, innovators, professionals and students for two days of knowledge sharing, research presentation and collaborative exploration.
